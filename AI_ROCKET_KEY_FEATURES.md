# AI Rocket - Key Features

## Core Features (Current)

### 1. Launch Points System (UPDATED)
Launch Points are part of the $5M AI Moonshot Challenge scoring criteria that measure how teams use AI to Run, Build, and Grow their business. Points are earned through three categories:
- **Launch Prep**: 50 points per level in Fuel, Boosters, and Guidance stages (up to 750 total)
- **Activity**: Points for daily AI usage, creating reports, and team collaboration (coming soon)
- **Milestones**: Bonus points for achieving team goals and business milestones (coming soon)
- **Icon**: Trophy
- **Color**: Amber-Orange Gradient
- **Status**: Active (v1.3)

### 2. Launch Preparation System
Guided 3-stage onboarding ensures proper setup before launching. Earn Launch Points as you progress through Fuel (add data), Boosters (use AI features), and Guidance (configure team) stages.
- **Icon**: Rocket
- **Color**: Orange-Blue Gradient
- **Status**: Active (v1.1)

### 3. Mission Control Dashboard
Track your progress, achievements, and Launch Points after launching. View personal and team stats, stage progress, and next milestones.
- **Icon**: Gauge
- **Color**: Cyan
- **Status**: Active (v1.1)

### 4. AI Data Sync & Connected Folders (ENHANCED)
Sync unlimited data from Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive/SharePoint, AND local file uploads to power the AI with your complete business knowledge. Connect up to 20 folders from any combination of providers.
- **Icon**: RefreshCw
- **Color**: Orange
- **Supported File Types**: Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDFs, Text files (.txt, .md, .csv, .json)
- **Features**:
  - **Unlimited Google Drive Sync**: Connect multiple folders and sync all your cloud documents
  - **Microsoft OneDrive/SharePoint Sync**: Connect Microsoft 365 cloud storage for enterprise teams
  - **Local File Upload**: Drag-and-drop any supported file directly into the platform
  - Real-time sync progress tracking with Fuel Level indicator
  - Flexible folder organization (up to 20 folders from any provider)
  - Incremental sync for efficient updates
  - Document management and deletion
  - Fuel Level progression based on total synced documents
  - Multi-provider support - use Google, Microsoft, or both simultaneously

## Preview Features (In Testing)

*Preview features are available to select users for testing. They will be rolled out to all users once testing is complete.*

### 25. Proactive Assistant (PREVIEW)
Your AI assistant evolves from reactive to proactive -- monitoring your business data, detecting deviations, and delivering insights before you ask. Wake up to a morning briefing of what happened overnight and what needs your attention.
- **Access**: Automatic delivery via enabled notification channels; configure in User Settings > Assistant Preferences
- **Icon**: Bell
- **Color**: Cyan-Blue Gradient
- **Status**: Preview
- **Features**:
  - **Overnight Insights ("While You Were Sleeping")**: AI analyzes your data overnight and delivers 2-6 insights each morning based on your proactive level setting
  - **Four-Lens Analysis**: Every insight is evaluated through Deviation Detection, Goal Alignment, Automation Opportunity, and Predictive Risk lenses
  - **Proactive Level Control**: Choose Low (essential only), Medium (balanced updates), or High (everything including daily summaries)
  - **Multi-Channel Delivery**: Receive insights via Email, SMS, WhatsApp, or Telegram -- configure any combination
  - **Quiet Hours**: Set do-not-disturb windows so notifications respect your schedule
  - **Notification Types**: 9 configurable notification triggers including Daily Summary, Report Ready, Goal Milestone, Meeting Reminder, Action Item Due, Team Mention, Insight Discovered, Sync Complete, and Weekly Recap
  - **Urgency-Based Triage**: Low-urgency items go to overnight summary; high-urgency items push immediately on all channels
  - **Custom Assistant Name**: Personalize your assistant's name in preferences
- **Impact**: Stay informed about your business without having to ask -- your AI works while you sleep

### 26. Scheduled Tasks & Reminders (PREVIEW)
Ask your AI assistant to schedule recurring or one-time tasks like research summaries, goal reviews, reminders, and data checks. The assistant executes tasks at the scheduled time and delivers results to you.
- **Access**: Ask your assistant in Agent Chat (e.g., "Remind me every Monday at 9am to review goals"), or view in Scheduled Tasks tab
- **Icon**: CalendarClock
- **Color**: Teal
- **Status**: Preview
- **Features**:
  - **Task Types**: Reminder, Research, Report, Goal Review, Data Check, Summary, and Custom Prompt
  - **Flexible Scheduling**: Once, Daily, Weekly, Biweekly, or Monthly recurrence options
  - **Delivery Methods**: Results delivered via in-app notification, Email, SMS, WhatsApp, or Telegram
  - **Task Management Dashboard**: View all tasks with status filters (Active, Paused, Completed), pause/resume tasks, and view execution history
  - **Execution Tracking**: See run count, next scheduled time, and detailed execution logs with success/failure status
  - **Natural Language Setup**: Just tell your assistant what you need and when -- no forms to fill out
  - **Context-Aware Execution**: Tasks run using your team's data and strategic priorities for relevant results
- **Impact**: Automate repetitive information gathering so you get the right insights at the right time without remembering to ask

### 29. Connected Apps & Integrations (PREVIEW)
Connect your favorite business tools and let AI work across all your data. A unified integration hub with 20+ integrations across 9 categories.
- **Access**: Connect page > Apps tab, or Mission Control > Connected Apps tile
- **Icon**: Plug
- **Color**: Blue-Teal Gradient
- **Status**: Preview (Phase 1 complete -- Calendar integrations active, more coming soon)
- **Features**:
  - **My Connections Tab**: Full management hub for all data connections. Admins can connect new providers (Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive), reconnect expired tokens, manage folders (add/remove via built-in folder browser), and disconnect providers with confirmation
  - Connected Apps dashboard showing all available integrations grouped by 9 categories: Storage, Calendar, Finance & Accounting, Communication, CRM & Sales, Project Management, Transcription, Analytics & Marketing, and Advanced (Custom)
  - Google Calendar integration -- one-click connect using existing Google Drive OAuth tokens
  - Outlook Calendar integration -- one-click connect using existing Microsoft OAuth tokens
  - AI agent automatically sees upcoming calendar events (3-day lookahead) and references them in conversation
  - Integration Registry with 20+ providers seeded across all categories
  - Connection status tracking (Connected, Expired, Error, Disconnected) with visual indicators
  - Usage analytics showing how many times the AI has used each connection
  - Read-only access by default with encrypted credential storage
  - One-click disconnect for any integration
- **Coming Soon Integrations**:
  - QuickBooks Online, Xero, Stripe (financial data)
  - Slack (team communication context)
  - HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel (CRM data)
  - Notion, Asana, Monday.com, Trello (project management)
  - Fireflies, Otter.ai (meeting transcription)
  - Zapier MCP (6000+ apps via MCP protocol)
- **Impact**: One place to connect and manage all your business tools so AI can work across all your data

### 30. MCP Tools & API Wizard (PREVIEW)
Discover, manage, and connect automation tools that power your AI assistant's capabilities. View available tools from connected MCP servers, monitor server health, and use the API Wizard to connect any custom API.
- **Access**: MCP Tools tab in Assistant Mode
- **Icon**: Wrench
- **Color**: Blue-Slate Gradient
- **Status**: Preview
- **Features**:
  - **Connected Servers Panel**: View all configured MCP servers (e.g., n8n) with health status monitoring (Healthy, Degraded, Unreachable)
  - **Available Tools Grid**: Browse all discovered automation tools with descriptions, categories, usage counts, and execution time metrics
  - **Tool Categories**: Finance, CRM, Communication, Project Management, Transcription, Marketing, Automation, General
  - **API Wizard** (Admin only): AI-powered step-by-step wizard to connect any custom API
    - Paste API documentation URL or text
    - AI automatically analyzes and discovers endpoints
    - Configure authentication (API Key, Bearer Token, Basic Auth)
    - Test connection before submitting
    - Endpoints generated and submitted for admin approval
  - **Tool Sync**: Admins can sync tools from MCP servers to discover new capabilities
  - **Search & Filter**: Full-text search and category filtering across all tools
  - **Audit Logging**: All tool executions are logged for compliance and monitoring
  - **Read-Only Indicators**: Clear badges showing which tools are read-only vs. write-capable
- **Impact**: Extend your AI assistant's capabilities beyond built-in integrations by connecting any API your business uses
